Earth Angel Beauty Salon was established in 2007 by none other than its owner, beauty and stylist professional, Danna Wilson. We are located at 2821 Ashland Road Columbia, SC 29210.

We have been servicing customers from all different backgrounds within the community and abroad. Contact or visit Earth Angel Beauty Salon today and we will take care of all your hair and cosmetic needs.
